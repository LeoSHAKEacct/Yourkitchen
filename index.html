How it works

Three roles,
one connected system.

01

Waiter takes the order

From their phone, the waiter selects the table, adds items from the menu, and submits. The order appears instantly on the kitchen display — no paper, no shouting.

02

Kitchen marks it done

The cook sees every order on the kitchen screen. As each dish is ready, they mark it done. The waiter gets a push notification to pick up — no guessing, no cold plates.

03

Manager sees everything

The dashboard shows table status, active orders, waiter performance, and revenue — all in real time. At end of month, one click generates the payroll report for the accountant.
